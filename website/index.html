smartphone school

Welcome to smartphone school!

It’s not as boring as it sounds. We promise.

smartphone school is a free, 7 day program that will help you evaluate your relationship with your phone and gain actionable steps to make your relationship healthier and more meaningful.

Our goal is not to get you to use your phone less (altough that might be a decision you make) or for you to become a Luddite. We simply want to give you tools to help you better understand how pervasive smartphones have become in our world and make decisions for yourself about what you want your relationship to be like with your smartphone.

We will do this by…

  • Educating you on the facts
  • Sharing articles from around the world
  • Giving you book reccomendations
  • Showing you video clips from smart people
  • Allowing you to share your own smartphone stories
